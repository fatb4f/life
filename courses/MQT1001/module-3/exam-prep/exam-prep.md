## Formula Recall (LaTeX)
- $\frac{a}{b}=a\div b$
- $\text{percent}=\text{decimal}\times 100\%$
- $\text{percent change}=\frac{\text{new}-\text{old}}{\text{old}}\times 100\%$

## Typical Pitfalls
- Dropping parentheses during substitution or distribution.
- Rounding too early; keep exact forms until the final step.
- Sign errors with negatives, especially in inequalities.
